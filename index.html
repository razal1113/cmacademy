Some common questions
we are often asked

What services does CM Academy provide?

CM Academy offers a wide range of educational programs, including:

-> Entrance coaching for competitive exams like NEET and JEE.
-> Higher secondary tuition for Science stream students, focusing on board exams.
-> A hybrid public school program that combines board exam preparation with entrance coaching for Science batch students.

What is the hybrid public school program at CM Academy?

The hybrid public school program is designed for Science batch students and integrates:

-> Board syllabus preparation with specialized coaching for competitive exams like NEET and JEE.
-> A combination of online and offline learning for maximum flexibility and efficiency.
-> Regular tests and progress tracking to ensure students excel in both board exams and entrance tests.

Does CM Academy offer a residential batch for repeat entrance coaching?

Yes, CM Academy provides a residential batch for students who wish to repeat their entrance exam preparation. This program includes:

-> On-campus accommodation and full-time mentoring.
-> A structured daily schedule with classes, self-study, and regular tests.
-> Comprehensive resources and support to help students improve their performance.

Who can enroll in the higher secondary tuition program at CM Academy?

The higher secondary tuition program is open to Science stream students in grades 11 and 12. The program is designed to help students:

-> Master their board exam syllabus.
-> Build a strong foundation for entrance exams.
-> Get personalized attention with doubt-clearing sessions and expert guidance.

What study materials and resources does CM Academy provide?

CM Academy provides:

->Detailed study materials for both board exams and competitive entrance exams.
->Access to mock tests, previous years’ papers, and practice questions.
->Mock tests and question papers prepared by teachers with 20+ years of experience in this field.

What makes CM Academy's programs accessible to all students?

CM Academy is committed to offering quality education that is accessible to everyone through:

->Affordable Pricing: Programs are designed to provide high value at competitive rates.
->Comprehensive Resources: Students receive access to detailed study materials, online resources, and mock tests to aid their preparation.
->Expert Guidance: Experienced faculty ensure personalized attention and support for every student to excel.
